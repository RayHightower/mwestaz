OUR STORY

Meridian West Properties

Meridian West Properties LLC creates office and commercial environments for the 21st century.

Our Mission

To enthusiastically develop real estate while adding lasting value for the individuals and communities impacted by our work. By devoting ourselves to constant, never-ending improvement, we strive to be innovators in planning, design and application, while exceeding expectations.

Our History

Meridian West Development, LLC was formed in 2003, but its roots were nurtured decades before in family businesses in Chicago and Connecticut. We are the culmination of over 30 years of experience in business and commercial real estate.

Our Focus

We believe in doing one thing, and doing it right. Our single focus is the development of commercial properties — efficient, attractive, comfortable and productive environments for business in the most desirable locations.

AZ/202

Chandler, Arizona — Gateway to Downtown

AZ/202 is Meridian West’s latest development, positioned at the Gateway to downtown Chandler. Located on a full diamond interchange at the northwest corner of Arizona Avenue & the Loop 202, this premier project provides outstanding visibility and access from the new SanTan Freeway.

The development offers a fantastic option for users looking to tap into the area’s skilled labor force and tremendous demographics. AZ/202 will contain a variety of product types that flow together and blend within one exciting, mixed-use development project.

Key Highlights
  • • Full diamond interchange access
  • • Outstanding visibility from SanTan Freeway
  • • Mixed-use development
  • • Strong demographics & skilled labor pool
  • • Prime Chandler location
